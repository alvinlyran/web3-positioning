# web3-positioning

Narrative and positioning frameworks for culture-driven Web3 growth.

---

## Overview

Most Web3 projects do not fail because of technology.

They fail because:
- nobody understands the vision
- the positioning is weak
- the narrative is forgettable
- the community lacks belief

This repository explores how modern Web3 brands can build stronger positioning, meaningful communities, and long-term cultural relevance.

---

---

## Core Philosophy

Attention attracts people.

Belief keeps them.

Culture makes them stay.

---

## Why This Matters

In Web3, technology alone is rarely enough.

The projects that survive market cycles usually understand:
- perception
- identity
- trust
- community behavior
- emotional connection

This repository documents frameworks and strategic insights around those ideas.
